AI Sleeping Agent Automatic Support

What the AI does automatically

  • detects repeated wrong answers
  • offers hints without waiting too long
  • changes to easier or harder questions automatically
  • generates extra practice when needed
  • saves voice answer success and progress
  • creates summaries for staff dashboards
This is designed to reduce routine interruption so parents do not need to step in for every small math problem.

Safe Limits

  • no raw audio recordings stored
  • no biometric voiceprints stored
  • no hidden surveillance
  • no unsafe autonomous decisions
  • major emotional, safety, or account issues still escalate to adults
The AI helper supports learning. It does not replace adult responsibility for safety, health, or serious concerns.
