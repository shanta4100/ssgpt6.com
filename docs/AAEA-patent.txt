AAEA – AI Air Energy Optimization System

Inventor: Arifur Shanta
Company: GNAIAAAC LLC

This system relates to energy optimization using airflow, temperature,
and AI-based control.

The system:
- monitors airflow and temperature
- identifies inefficiencies
- provides optimization strategies
- supports system continuity during outages

The system may be implemented as:
- software
- control systems
- integrated industrial solutions

The innovation includes:
- airflow-based optimization logic
- AI-controlled system adjustments
- modular deployment
