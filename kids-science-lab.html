Automated AI Help Center

What the AI can handle by itself

  • repeat instructions
  • explain one step at a time
  • show a simpler example
  • open a hint card
  • switch to easy mode
  • read the lesson out loud
  • move a child to the next safe step
This helps with normal learning frustration so kids can keep going without needing routine adult help for every small step.

What still should escalate to a parent or teacher

  • account issues
  • payments or subscriptions
  • device safety problems
  • major emotional distress
  • anything involving health or physical safety
The AI helper is for learning support, not emergency or health decision-making.
