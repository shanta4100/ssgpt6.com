Child Safety & Parent Permission Policy

Parent Permission Required:
Children can only use this platform with parent, guardian, or teacher approval.

Age Verification:
Age confirmation may be required before access is granted.

Closed System:
No external websites or search engines are allowed in child learning mode.

Allowed Activities:

  • Reading
  • Educational games
  • Avatar lessons
  • Voice learning tools

1 Hour Limit:
The child learning session automatically ends after 1 hour.

Cooldown:
Access is locked for 4 hours unless unlocked by a parent or guardian.

School Hours Lock:
Child mode can be blocked during school hours.

Privacy:
No raw audio or biometric voice data is required for standard child learning support.
